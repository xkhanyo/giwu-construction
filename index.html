About Giwu Construction

Founded in 2021 by Nolukhanyo Ngceni, Giwu Construction brings together expertise in project management, quantity surveying, construction health & safety, and on-site supervision. Our mission is to manage projects efficiently, deliver excellent value, and maintain the highest professional standards.

Founder & CEO

Nolukhanyo — Founder & CEO

Nolukhanyo is the founder and CEO of Giwu Construction. She holds an Advanced Diploma in Quantity Surveying (NQF L7), Advanced Diploma in Construction Health & Safety (NQF L7), Diploma in Building (NQF L6), NCV L4 Civil Engineering & Building Construction, and an NQF 8 Postgraduate Diploma in Construction. She also has strong academic and practical experience in project management, bringing leadership and technical excellence to every project.

Her multidisciplinary background in construction, safety, quantity surveying, and project management positions her as a reliable leader capable of delivering high-standard results with transparency, precision, and commitment.
